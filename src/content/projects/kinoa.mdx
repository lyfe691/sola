# A Cleaner Way to Watch

**Kinoa** is a free streaming site for films and series. No subscription needed — browse, search, save to your watchlist, and watch right on the detail page. It runs ads to keep the lights on, pulls metadata from TMDB, and streams through VidFast with a clean interface that gets out of your way.

## What You Get

The home page opens with a search bar front and center, a trending section with filter tabs (All / Movies / TV Shows), and rows for latest releases. Everything is filterable and loads live from TMDB.

Every title has its own detail page — poster, backdrop, rating, runtime, genres, episode list for TV. The player embeds inline so you never leave. If playback fails, just pick a different server.

## Under the Hood

Data fetching is all server-side via typed helpers in `lib/tmdb.ts`. TMDB responses are cached through the App Router's fetch layer. The player is a single `player.tsx` component that takes a discriminated union — `kind: "movie"` or `kind: "tv"` — and builds a VidFast iframe URL accordingly.

Auth and persistence run through **Supabase**: watchlist entries and watch history are stored per-user, with a backup/restore feature on the watchlist page (you can see it in the screenshot above).

Ads are served via three components in `components/ads/`: a native banner that appears between content rows on the home page, a popunder, and a social bar.

## Mobile

The layout is fully responsive. Navigation collapses to a hamburger, the grid drops to two columns, and search/watchlist work the same on any screen size.
